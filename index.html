Synopsis- The series follows the adventures of ted mosby and his love life as a singlw man.
Characters: Ted mosby Kind, passionate, hopeful, and a little dorky. Ted isent as smooth as barney or as adorable as marshall, but his charm lies in his quick wit and his deep dedication to the people he loves.
Characters: Barney stinson He is manipulative, oversexed buisnessman in his thirties who always wears a suit, and likes women. He came in to save everyones day and the connections cannot be matched in the group.
character: Robin sherbatsky Robin is on and off love interest in barneystinson and ted mosby, and close friends with lily aldrin and marshall erikson.
character: Lily aldrin Extremely loyal and caring, but with a lot of quirks. Though she may seem small and innocent, Lily is incapable of keeping a secret (“Don’t tell me things!”), loves to gossip, has a secret shopping addiction, is always snooping into other people’s business, and is a self-proclaimed “extremely sexual person.”
Characters: Marshall erikson Marshall is very polite since he has grown up in Minnesota. He is loving and caring and confident and extremely emotional, which he often gets teased for by the gang. He is very passionate about what he does, and cares deeply for his family and friends.
